Project Delivery Services

Talk to your regional expert

Jeremy Rae

Principal and Project Delivery Services Service Line Lead, Apac

Contact Jeremy

Large project delivery is becoming increasingly complex. One single delay can have a ripple effect on the entire project outcome.

Helping clients create long-term value

Advisian understands the specific commercial, technical and collaboration challenges associated with large infrastructure and resource projects. We provide advice on project delivery improvements, helping clients create long-term value in developing and managing their assets.

In addition to this, our consultants provide extensive senior managerial and operational experience to help clients with their capital asset investments and portfolios. We work across the public and private sector and all project delivery models involving hard dollar, alliances, early contractor involvement, public private partnerships and build, own, operate and transfer schemes.

Our services include:

  • Project delivery

  • Strategy contracting models and risk allocation

  • Scope definition

  • Development and optimisation

  • Design management

  • Planning and scheduling

  • Commercial management

  • Risk management
